Yam and Fried Eggs

Description

In major parts of the world, various species of yam are the favorite goto source for energy and nutritional value.
This makes it's combination with another highly nutritious food a No-brainer

Eggs, another very important source of protein is easily found and cheaply available. This makes Yam and Egg the fastest meal you can make at home.

Ingredients

  • Tubers of Yam
  • 3 pieces of Eggs
  • Groundnut or Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the yam
  2. Mix the eggs with pepper and salt
  3. Fry mixture with groundnut oil
  4. Finally, serve together
