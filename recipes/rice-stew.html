Rice and Stew

Description

In major parts of the world, Rice with saucy stew has become the favorite goto source for breakfast energy and nutritional value.
It's availabity as a grainy food makes it's combination with saucy spicey stew a No-brainer

Stew, acting as a taste booster due to the prevalence of pepper is a very good compliment for Rice

Ingredients

  • Cups Rice
  • Grounded Pepper
  • Groundnut or Vegetable oil
  • Salt, spices

Steps

  1. Boil the Rice
  2. Set the groudnut oil on fire
  3. After some time, pour the pepper into the pot of hot oil
  4. Add your spicys as you wish and leave for 10 mins
  5. Finally, serve as you wish
