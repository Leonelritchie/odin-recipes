Potato Porridge

Description

Potato Porridge is a delicious delicacy very popular for it's versatility
It's very delicious and fiber nature makes it a great option for those who want something sweet to take.

Ingredients

  • Potato tuber
  • Grounded Pepper
  • Palm oil
  • Salt, spices

Steps

  1. Slice potato into boiling water
  2. Pour palm oil into boiling potato
  3. After a while, pour pepper boiling content
  4. Add your spices as you wish and leave for 10 mins
  5. Finally, serve as you wish
